  • The Establishment of Alumni Association

    CUHK, Shenzhen Finance Club

    The Finance club from CUHK-Shenzhen was founded in late 2016 and supported by SME. It is a club facing all students loving finance. Furthermore, CFC will hold online and offline activities regularly, such as company visits, career planning lectures, business competitions, consultancy teaching and so on, inviting professionals in the financial industry to share information and guide students’ learning.

    CUHK, Shenzhen Consulting Club

    CCC is committed to become a top business talents gathering place, positioning elite club of CUHK, Shenzhen. Regular activities include consulting related lectures, case training, consultation report writing exercises. Moreover, CCC will organize social activities with club members and advisors in consulting industry for free communication, as well as promote internship information sharing platform. CCC is dedicated to making all members reach the standards of consulting industry, and get internship offer or academic offer in foreign investment consulting firms or above the level.

    The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen Alumni Chapters

    The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen Alumni Chapters are regional alumni organization established in Hong Kong, the United States, Canada, Europe , etc. Alumni, at home and abroad, spontaneously set up local alumni associations. These alumni chapters organize various activities according to their actual conditions to achieve mutual help among local alumni. The alumni chapters are the bridges between the alumni and our university. They gather the power of the alumni and provide help for the alumni in the same area in terms of accommodation, living, study, work, etc.

  • For now, there is already a group of CUHK-Shenzhen alumni, who, with their knowledge and dreams, have organized various social events in different countries and regions. Wherever you are, you are welcomed by friendly faces. CUHK-Shenzhen alumni will be the lifelong imprint of our graduates and the most impressive symbol for the university!
