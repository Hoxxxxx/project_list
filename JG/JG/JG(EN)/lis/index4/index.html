      ACCA

      The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) is the global professional accounting body offering the Chartered Certified Accountant qualification (ACCA or FCCA). In March 2018, ACCA recorded that it has 208,000 members and 503,000 students in 179 countries.

      http://cn.accaglobal.com

      CFA

      CFA, short for Chartered Financial Analyst, is the strictest and high-standard qualification in the global investment industry. Known as the first exam in finance, it has set the norms and criteria for the global investment industry in terms of ethics, professional standards and knowledge systems.

      Qualication of applicant: no requirement for major with a bachelor's degree or an equivalent professional level; those who have a total of four years of study combined with full-time work experience; if the applicant does not have a bachelor's degree, but an equivalent professional level, he/she will also be accepted as a candidate.College students may register for the exam within 12 months before graduation.

      http://www.cfa.cn

      CICPA

      CICPA is short for Chinese Institute of the Certified Public Accountants.CICPA membership is awarded to those who have taken the National Unified Examination for Certified Public Accountant and passed all the subjects, and have had their application approved.

      Qualication of applicant: Chinese citizens with a higher college degree or above, or with intermediate or above technical titles in accounting (or related majors) may sign up for the Special Part of the National Unified Examination for CPA; after obtaining the certificate for passing this examination, they may sign up for the Comprehensive Part of the National Unified Examination for CPA.

      http://www.cicpa.org.cn

      HKICPA

      The Hong Kong Institute of Certified Public Accountants (the Institute) is the only body authorized by law to register and grant practising certificates to certified public accountants in Hong Kong. The Institute has more than 42,000 members and more than 18,000 registered students. Members of the Institute are entitled to the description "certified public accountant" and to the designation CPA.

      http://www.hkicpa.org.hk/en/

      CPA

      CPA refers to the person who has obtained the certificate of certified public accountant and practiced in an accounting firm. The full name of CPA is Certified Public Accountant in English, which is abbreviated as CPA. CPA refers to the professionals engaged in social auditing/intermediary auditing/independent auditing. CPA is the only officially recognized certified public accountant qualification in China and the only professional qualification with the right of signature.

      http://cpaexam.cicpa.org.cn/

      CPSM

      The CPSM® is the gold standard of supply management credentials. It is proof that you have mastered specific core competencies and can apply them in your daily role to create winning outcomes. Find out how a CPSM® can catapult you to greater success.

      www.instituteforsupplymanagement.org

      PMP

      The Project Management Professional (PMP) ® is the most important industry-recognized certification for project managers. You can find PMPs leading projects in nearly every country and, unlike other certifications that focus on a particular geography or domain, the PMP ® is truly global.

      www.pmi.org

      ECBA

      The Entry Certificate in Business Analysis™ (ECBA™) is a place to start to become a Business Analyst. ECBA covers foundational knowledge on how to approach business analysis according to the BABOK® Guide.

      https://www.iiba.org

      FRM

      Recognized in every major market, the FRM is the leading certification for risk managers. It is consistently in demand by nearly every major bank and firm in the world, and is awarded only to professionals who demonstrate the knowledge and ability to anticipate, respond, and adapt to critical risk issues.

      http://www.cfa.cn

    • The Shenzhen government entrusts CUHK (SZ) with the funds and resources to establish the Shenzhen Finance Institute. The institute aims to provide a globally recognized center in the field of economics and finance that nurtures innovative talents; cultivates an advanced global research base; builds international academic exchange platforms; and serves as a strategic planning think-tank.

      Shenzhen Finance Institute CUHK (SZ) currently offers education programs that include the Master of Science in Finance, Master of Science in Economics, Master of Science in Accounting, Master of Science in Data Science and Executive Education. In the future, it will admit and foster the master and doctoral students in more relevant fields of economics and finance.

      Master of Science in Finance (full-time)

      The Master of Science in Finance program provides financial principles, quantitative analysis capabilities and comprehensive professional training combined with practical applications.The course is taught in English. Students may choose full-time (including advanced financial research) or part-time study.Using a unique way of academic mentor plus industry mentor, we not only help students improve their academic theory, but also keep up with the latest developments and changes in the market and understand the most cutting-edge information.

      After graduation, students are expected to acquire various skills and methods widely used in the financial industry, which lays a solid foundation for them to enter the financial industry and become competitive professionals in financial markets of China and the world.

      Master of Science in Finance (part-time)

      The Master of Science in Finance program (part-time) provides financial principles, quantitative analysis capabilities and comprehensive professional training combined with practical applications, contributing to the further development of on-the-job people in the financial and related fields such as securities, banking, financial management, and consulting.

      After graduation, students are expected to acquire various skills and methods widely used in the financial industry, which lays a solid foundation for them to enter the financial industry and become competitive high-end and innovative talents in financial markets of China and the world.

      Master of Science in Economics

      The Master of Science in Economics program provides financial principles, quantitative analysis capabilities and comprehensive professional training combined with practical applications, helping students make a well-preparation for career development in economic fields such as private financial institutions, government departments such as central banks, international trading companies, stock exchanges, and consulting agencies.After graduation, students are expected to acquire the skills and methods widely used in a number of financial sectors, like macroeconomic analysis of the financial industry, policy analysis of government departments, and quantitative analysis of the consulting industry, laying a solid foundation for them to enter the economic area and become competitive professionals in China and even the world.

      Master of Science in Accounting

      The Master of Science in Accounting program aims to cultivate multi-disciplinary talents with global perspective, systematic knowledge of accounting-related fields, analytical and practical application capabilities, and in-depth understanding of the China's reform in capital market operations. Focusing on the academic and practical combination, this course provides a tailor-made international environment and a broad employment prospect for students (accounting or non-accounting majors) who are interested in the accounting, finance, banking, consulting and management.The course is taught in English under a 24-month system.

      Master of Science in Data Science

      Through this 1.5-year Master of Science in Data Science program, graduates will acquire the cutting-edge big data processing technologies and learn the frontier theories and methods that underpin big data technologies.In addition, students, before graduation, will participate in an actual research project to show their innovative methods and applications in the fields of business, government, security, medicine, biology, natural sciences and environment by using skillfully the basic concepts of data science.

      Executive Education

      The Executive Education of Shenzhen Finance Institute, relying on the Institute's strong financial teaching strength and ability to integrate international education faculty, offers open courses for executives and customized courses for a business or financial institution, delivering valuable learning plans and solutions to executives in corporate and financial institutions.Shenzhen Finance Institute is dedicated to building an international platform that integrates the past and the present, and the Oriental and the Western, so as to broaden the international financial horizons and meet the latest challenges, and improve the quality of financial business, inspire new thinking, enhance the performance and efficiency, training a group of modern business leaders with a global vision, a deep understanding of the status and trend of financial industry, and outstanding professionalism and innovation capabilities.

      If you need more information, please go to the website of Shenzhen Finance Institute: http://sfi.cuhk.edu.cn

    • GMAT

      The Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT) is a computer adaptive test (CAT) intended to assess certain analytical, writing, quantitative, verbal, and reading skills in written English for use in admission to a graduate management program, such as an MBA.

      https://www.mba.com

      GRE

      An admissions exam that measures the verbal reasoning, quantitative reasoning, critical thinking and analytical writing skills of candidates applying to graduate and business schools. Institutions use scores, along with other measures, to identify the best candidates for their programs, and for placement, program evaluation and selection of fellowship recipients.

      http://www.ets.org/gre/

      QS World University Rankings

      QS World University Rankings is an annual publication of university ranking by Quacquarelli Symonds (QS). Being the only international ranking to have received International Ranking Expert Group (IREG) approval, the QS ranking is viewed as one of the three most-widely read university rankings in the world, along with Academic Ranking of World Universities and Times Higher Education World University Rankings.

      https://www.topuniversities.com/

      TOEFL

      TOEFL(Test of English as a Foreign Language)is a standard test to measure the English language ability of non-native speakers wishing to enroll in English-speaking universities. The test is accepted by many English-speaking academic and professional institutions. TOEFL is one of the two major English-language tests in the world, the other being the IELTS.

      http://www.ets.org/toefl

      IELTS

      IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is an international standard test of English language proficiency for non-native English language speakers. IELTS is one of the major English-language tests in the world, which is accepted by most Australian, British, Canadian and New Zealand academic institutions, by over 3,000 academic institutions in the United States, and by various professional organizations across the world.

      https://www.ielts.org/
