Mentor Program
The Career Mentorship program for undergraduates of the School of Management and Economics, CUHK-Shenzhen aims to provide alumni and students with a reciprocal platform for ideas and experience exchange, so as to improve their professional competitiveness, broaden their visions and help them keep pace with the times.Participants are expected to gain:
  • Extensive network
  • Leadership fostering
  • In-depth learning and mutual help
  • Longstanding friendship
Activity Recommendations

Academic activities：

  • Share book reading
  • Participate in seminars and workshops together
  • Discuss the current events
  • Instructions and exchanges in expert fields

Social activities

  • Company visit
  • Daily work guidance and exchange of ideas
  • Career "shadow" plan
  • Share career planning and insights
  • Industry trends and dynamic analysis
  • Establish career connections
About Mentors

Selection Criteria

  1. CUHK alumni or senior management in the industry, with more than 5 years of experience
  2. Practitioners in banking, FMCG, consulting, technology and other related industry or self-employed entrepreneurs
  3. Those with training experience or who have served as mentors are preferred
  4. Those with strong sense of responsibility

Mentor's Role

  • Teacher
  • Role model
  • Instructor
  • Inspirator
  • Friend & supporter
