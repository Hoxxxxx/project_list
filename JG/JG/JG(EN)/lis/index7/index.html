bout Alumni Affairs - School of Management and Economics, CUHK-Shenzhen：

The School of Management and Economics, CUHK-Shenzhen puts a premium on is committed to building and fostering the alumni relations and supporting the further studies and career development of graduates. In 2018, Alumni Affairs - School of Management and Economics, CUHK-Shenzhen was established, aiming to serve as a link between alumni and the school and provide services to alumni based on the Career Center.

Alumni Affairs will organize online and offline activities from time to time, such as alumni day, alumni dialogue and mentor program, to strengthen the relationships between alumni and school mentors and help alumni expand their social connections. Alumni can access the resources of the Career Center through Alumni Affairs, such as industry information sharing sessions, one-to-one consultations and online learning resources, to enhance their professional attainment and competitiveness.
