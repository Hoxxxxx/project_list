2018 "Teacher & Friend"Career Mentorship Program Closing Ceremony for Career Mentor & Alumni Party Reunion of School of Management and Economics

Time：19:00-21:30，2018-11-16

Venue: F2, Library, CUHK-Shenzhen

Sign up：12/30 Sign up

With its increasingly high international fame and continuous expansion of international partners, CUHK-Shenzhen is beginning to establish a growing number of international cooperation projects and show diversified characteristics. In the first half of 2018, the school signed 16 new cooperation projects with 14 top universities from 8 countries.,and 69 cooperative institutions abroad. As of now, our partner universities at abroad is up to 69.

According to these cooperation agreements, the school university should carry outwill launch a series of programs, such as student exchange, overseas exchange, summer program, 3+1+N undergraduate + master educationcombined bachelor/master program and 4+1 undergraduate + master studycombined bachelor/master program with the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies (Switzerland), Bocconi University (Italy), University of Padua (Italy), University of Konstanz (Germany), Columbia University (USA), University of California, Los Angeles (USA), University of California, Riverside (USA), University of Wisconsin-Madison (USA), University of Texas at Dallas (USA), Autonomous University of Mexico (Mexico), Nagoya University of Commerce (Japan), Toyo University (Japan), Yonsei University (Korea), Mahidol University (International College)

IHEID (Institut de hautes études internationales et du développement) has a high level of research and international prestige in the fields of international relations, international law, international economics, history of international relations and the science of development. This In March, 2018, we signed with IHEID an overseas exchange project agreement, which allows the two parties to send two postgraduates each year to study at the other school.

Founded in 1966, the University of Konstanz is a well-known public university in Germany.At the beginning of establishment, this reform-oriented university has diverged itself from the traditional form of division by combining different faculties into an interdisciplinary college. This In March, 2018, we signed with the University of Konstanzan an overseas exchange project agreement, which allows the two parties to send two postgraduates or undergraduates each year to study at the other school.

Following the "4+1" undergraduate + master studycombined bachelor/master program between our school and Columbia University's School of International and Public Affairs (SIPA) in 2017, our cooperation scope should will expand to the School of Professional Studies (SPS) this year. Under the agreement, outstanding students of our School of Management and Economics may are allowed to apply for SPS's one-year master program in the next semester of their junior year 3, including applied analytics, bioethics, architectural engineering management, enterprise risk management, and non-profit management and strategic communication. Qualified students should enterwill be directly admitted by Columbia University directly after their four-year undergraduate study.

The University of California, Riverside is one of the top 10 universities in the University of California System, the world's most influential public university system. Its business school is the largest of the 10 campuses in California, equipped with the most influential AACSB certification in global management education.CUHK-Shenzhen and the Business School have reached an agreement that allows the two parties to carry out the "3+1 + N" undergraduate + master educationcombined bachelor/master program. This program is open to the undergraduates of different majors in the School of Management and Economics or of sStatistics majors in the School of Science and TechnologyEngineering.
